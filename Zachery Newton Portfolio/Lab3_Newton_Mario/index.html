Mario Mapping

Zach Newton, GISP

This explainer describes a web map based on the classic Nintendo Game Super Mario Bros III. Unmute the audio below to get in the spirit of Mario.

The map uses the classic Nintendo labeling in its water labeling and inverses the color scheme to work in the mapbox environment .

In-depth Cartographic Research

❮ ❯

Berlin Germany takes the pathways of the worldmap in game and recreates them using the roads. This was accomplished using multiple layers of differing strokes to create a fill effect. In addition to the pathways, we also use the powerblocks from the game as our POIs with their text appearing in gold to simulate the coins for breaking regular blocks.

Yellowstone National Park, as well as other national park areas, uses the textures of the background vegetation from the gameplay.

Looking at Utah, we start to see the Cities represented by the classic Mario coins.

Looking at Southeast Asia shows us the Country labeling done in the Title font from the Game's cover art.
